Recommendations

Recommendations

Here are some tips to help you lower your carbon footprint in your daily life.

Energy & Household Usage

  • Switch to energy-efficient LED light bulbs to reduce electricity consumption by up to 80%.
  • Unplug electronics and chargers when not in use to combat "vampire" power consumption.
  • Wash your clothes in cold water. About 90% of a washing machine's energy goes to heating the water.

Screen Time & AI

  • Adjust your screen's brightness. A brighter screen uses more energy and can strain your eyes.
  • Choose more energy-efficient AI models or use them during off-peak hours if possible.
